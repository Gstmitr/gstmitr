Simple Process

How It Works

Everything happens on WhatsApp. No app to install. No office visit needed.

1

Choose Your Language

Send "Hi" to our WhatsApp number. The bot shows a menu of 23 languages. Select yours — every message from this point arrives in your chosen language.

2

Tell Us Your Business Type

Select Proprietorship, Partnership, Pvt Ltd, or any other entity type. The bot immediately sends you the exact document checklist for your type — in your language.

3

Send Your Documents

Photograph your PAN card, Aadhaar, bank document, and address proof. Send all photos at once in any order. The bot reads each one automatically using AI and tells you what is still needed.

4

Review and Confirm

The bot shows you all collected details in your language for review. Correct anything with a simple reply. Then confirm — the bot runs 5 automatic document checks.

5

Pay via UPI

Pay using PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, or any UPI app. Send a payment screenshot — the bot verifies it automatically. No payment gateway fees.

6

Receive Your GSTIN

The bot fills the GST portal form on your behalf. You complete one Aadhaar OTP. Your GSTIN arrives on WhatsApp in 3–7 working days — in your language.
